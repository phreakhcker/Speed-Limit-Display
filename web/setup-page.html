Quick Start Guide

1

Get a TomTom API Key (free)

Go to developer.tomtom.com, create a free account, then go to Dashboard > My Apps > Add New App. Copy your API key. Free tier includes 2,500 requests per day.

2

Connect the Device

Plug your Speed Limit Display into your computer using a USB-C cable. Make sure you use a data cable, not a charge-only cable.

3

Flash the Firmware

Click the "Install Speed Limit Display" button above. Select your device from the popup and wait for the installation to complete (about 30 seconds).

4

Connect to Setup WiFi

After flashing, the device creates a WiFi hotspot called SpeedLimit-Setup. Connect to it with your phone. A setup page will open automatically.

5

Enter Your Details

On the setup page, enter your WiFi network name, WiFi password, and TomTom API key. The device will save these and restart.

6

Mount and Drive

Place the device on your dashboard. It will connect to WiFi, lock onto GPS satellites, and start showing speed limits within 30-60 seconds. The display turns green when you're under the limit, yellow when close, and red when over.

Troubleshooting

The "Install" button doesn't appear

Make sure you're using Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge on a desktop computer. Safari and Firefox do not support Web Serial. Mobile browsers are also not supported.

No device shows up when I click Connect

Try a different USB-C cable — some cables are charge-only and don't carry data. Also try a different USB port on your computer. On Windows, you may need to install CP2102 drivers or CH340 drivers.

The device won't connect to my WiFi

Make sure you're connecting to a 2.4 GHz network (the ESP32 does not support 5 GHz WiFi). Double-check your WiFi password. The device will keep trying to connect — if it fails, it will re-open the setup hotspot after 60 seconds.

GPS is not getting a fix

GPS needs a clear view of the sky. Make sure the device is near a window or on the dashboard. The first GPS lock can take 30–60 seconds outdoors, or up to 5 minutes indoors. The GPS icon will blink while searching and turn solid when locked.

Speed limit shows "--"

This means the device has GPS but hasn't received speed limit data yet. Check that your WiFi is connected (WiFi icon should be solid), your TomTom API key is valid, and you're on a road that TomTom has data for. Rural or private roads may not have speed limit data.

How do I update the firmware later?

Just come back to this page, plug in your device, and click the Install button again. Your WiFi and API settings are saved separately and won't be erased during a firmware update.

How do I reset to factory settings?

Hold the Boot button while pressing Reset. This clears all saved settings and restarts the setup hotspot.
